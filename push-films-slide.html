Greg Hobden

Film Producer · London

Push Films is the production vehicle of Greg Hobden — a London-based film producer with thirty-five years of experience across theatrical documentary, high-end commercial production, large-scale live events, and corporate film.

A significant part of that work has been on-site event and conference coverage — executive interviews, vox pops, delegate footage, and fast-turnaround edits. Much of this work is confidential by nature. The clients who commission it value discretion as much as craft.
