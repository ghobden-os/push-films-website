Greg Hobden

Film Producer

Push Films is the production vehicle of Greg Hobden — a London-based film producer with thirty-five years of experience across theatrical documentary, high-end commercial production, and large-scale live events. Credits include three theatrical features for Universal Pictures, a decade of automotive work for Rolls-Royce, Jaguar Land Rover, Ford and McLaren, and nearly a decade producing all film and media for the UAE's pavilions at three World Expos.
