Profile

Thirty years in film. Big screens, small screens, global brands and live audiences.

I came up through the crew. Runner, 2nd AD, First AD, Producer. Good filmmaking is built on preparation. The work nobody sees. I learned that early on, and it has never left me.

Three theatrical feature documentaries for Universal Pictures. A decade of automotive work for Rolls-Royce, Jaguar Land Rover, McLaren and Ford. Nearly a decade producing for the UAE's pavilions at three successive World Expos. Twelve weeks at 6,000 metres on Everest, managing camera teams embedded with the British Army's attempt on the West Ridge. More people have died on that route than have reached the summit.

Live production has become an increasing specialism.

Every project has its own challenges. The size of the budget doesn't change that. A high one brings its own complexity. A low one demands different thinking. The expectation of what gets delivered doesn't change either way.
